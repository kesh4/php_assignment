HEALTH FIRST

Health First is the first ever Government corporate GYM in Sri Lanka.

It is a full-equipped Government gym with a range of Cybex USA fitness equipment for a complete workout.

Treadmills, arc trainers, and bike are the cardio machines, while the multifunctional Bravo Functional Training System complete with accessories that include short bars, ropes and straps can render 100 different exercises.
